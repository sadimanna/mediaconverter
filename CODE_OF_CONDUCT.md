# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
MediaConverter project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all.

Regardless of:

- experience level
- gender identity
- sexual orientation
- disability
- ethnicity
- nationality
- religion
- technical background

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants in this community are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the project
- Be collaborative and helpful
- Use welcoming and inclusive language

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any form of intimidation

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action when violations occur
- Ensuring a respectful project environment

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the repository maintainer.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
